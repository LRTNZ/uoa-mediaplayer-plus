## Features
- Download button for watching lectures offline
- Keyboard shortcuts:
  - 'J' and 'L' for seeking
  - 'K' to play/pause
  - 'F' to go fullscreen
  - '>' to speed up and '<' to slow down, '/' to reset speed
- Automatically skip copyright notice
- Remember where you stopped watching
- Various other fixes and improvements
